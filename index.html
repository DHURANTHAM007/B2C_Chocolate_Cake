Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour into greased and floured cake pans.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Cool before serving.
